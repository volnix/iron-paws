About

Iron Paws is a company that is all about making creative designs for the everyday use.

From the interior of your home, to the exterior, and even for those furry family members, Iron Paws covers it all. Railings, doors, furniture and custom raised pet feeding stands are just a few of the things Iron Paws creates.

If you have a vision of your own, that is even better. These creations are made to last and to be functional in any home. Let Iron Paws make a lasting impression on your home, family, and pets!
